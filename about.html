Meet The Team
03
WELCOME TO
THE FAMILY

My name is Bryan, I am the owner and sole developer of Rain City Websites. I got into programming after my wife had our first child. I was and still am a stay-at-home dad, and I wanted a career that I can do from home and still be there with the kids to watch them grow up. So over the course of two years I taught myself how to code websites the right way by hand and just started calling small businesses to offer my help.

What I found was that a lot of small businesses don't have the funds to spend thousands of dollars upfront on a new website. And the ones that did, got taken advantage of and have a terrible website that looks like it was built by someone in a dungeon and haven't seen design trends for the last 10 years.

Bryan
Owner & Developer
